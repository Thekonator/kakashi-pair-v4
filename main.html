🔥How to Pair Ancore🔥

  1. Click on Get Pair Code to get a 6-digit code for your WhatsApp account.
  2. Open your WhatsApp and tap on Linked Devices.
  3. Tap on Link a Device and enter the code you received.
  4. Done! Your session will be connected and ready to use in Ancore 🔥.
